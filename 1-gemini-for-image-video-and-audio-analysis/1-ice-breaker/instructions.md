# Nano Banana - Generating a Headshot 

## Overview
This play teaches you how to use Gemini’s Nano Banana 2 model to perform image generation within the fictitious corporate ecosystem of Cymbal-Mart.

### Objectives
In this play, you learn how to:
- Use a personal headshot as a reference to place yourself into a high-fidelity, industrial warehouse setting.
- Combine specific branding elements to create a professional, context-aware digital assets to bridge the gap between personal identity and corporate storytelling.

## Instructions
1. Open Gemini Enterprise in your browser.

2. In the chat bar, select the Tools icon and then choose Generate images (NB2).

3. Select + Add files and choose Upload files.

4. In the dialog, select headshot.jpg click Open.

5. In the chat, copy and paste the following prompt, then press ENTER.

```text
You are a professional photographer, taking a gritty and authentic portrait for an employee spotlight series. Using the face from the headshot.jpg, maintaining the facial identity as a perfect, unaltered match, place it onto the body of a worker.
The worker is wearing a branded high-visibility safety vest (neon yellow and blue) with a "Cymbal-Mart" logo on the chest, a standard blue uniform shirt, and dark work trousers. Their expression is competent and professional, holding a hand-held scanner gun in one hand and resting the other on a large metal rolling inventory cart filled with boxes.
The setting is the interior of a massive Cymbal-Mart physical warehouse distribution center. The background is composed of endless rows of industrial, floor-to-ceiling metal shelving stocked high with cardboard boxes labeled with "Cymbal-Mart" branding and barcodes. In the distance, other workers are busy operating forklifts, and large "RECEIVING" or "AISLE 4B" signs hang from the high steel beams. The lighting is functional and realistic, mixing harsh overhead fluorescent lights with natural light coming from large loading bay doors on the side. The photo is taken at eye level, capturing the scale of the facility. The overall feel is industrious, efficient, and realistic. The photo should be sharp and detailed, looking like a documentary-style image.
```
6. Save your final result for sharing.
