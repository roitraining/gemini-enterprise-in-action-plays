# Back to School Infographic

## Overview
This play teaches you how to use Gemini’s Nano Banana 2 model to transform promotional data into a high-fidelity, conceptual infographic to bridge the gap between raw marketing strategy and persuasive visual storytelling.

### Objectives
In this play, you learn how to direct the AI to synthesize complex promotional details into a professional digital asset.


## Instructions
1. Open Gemini Enterprise in your browser.

2. In the chat bar, select the Tools icon and then choose Generate images (NB2).

3. In the chat, copy and paste the following prompt, then press ENTER.

```
You are a data visualization artist who creates infographics with dramatic, physical metaphors. Create a striking, *horizontal* infographic-style image promoting the "Cymbal-Mart: Back-to-School Super Sale." The scene is a dramatic, split landscape. On the left side, labeled **"THE AUGUST MOUNTAIN,"** stands a massive, precarious, towering pile of crumpled homework, worn-out backpacks, broken pencils, and dull grays, rendered with a visual weight. A sign on this pile reads: **"$1,200+ National Average Spend (2025)."** A cartoon mother figure below looks overwhelmed and exhausted, shielding her eyes. On the right side, labeled **"THE CYMBAL-MART SOLUTION,"** the same mountain has been replaced by a sprawling, organized *city* made entirely of fresh, colorful school supplies—notebook skyscrapers, pencil-case trains, and textbook bridges, all gleaming with primary colors. It is vibrant and inviting. An energetic young student, wearing a new, customized "Cymbal-Mart Approved" backpack, runs gleefully toward the city, pulling a wagon stacked with glowing deals labeled **"50% OFF"** and **"$1 BACK-TO-SCHOOL DEALS."** A large, friendly Cymbal-Mart logo is integrated seamlessly as the central archway entrance to this city. Along the bottom of the entire scene, clean data callouts read: **"$350+ Average Cymbal-Mart Family Savings,"** **"1,000+ Items Under $5,"** and **"Same-Day Store Pickup."** The final banner text across the bottom of the entire image reads: **"Cymbal-Mart: Elevate Their Future. Lighten Your Load."**
```

3. Review the output and refine the prompt for clarity.

4. Save your final result for sharing.
