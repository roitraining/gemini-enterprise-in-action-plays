# Gemini for Image, Video, and Audio Analysis

## What students learn
Students learn how to use Gemini and related Google AI tools to create and analyze visual and audio content for business outcomes. Across the plays, they practice image generation, infographic creation, meta prompting for stronger visual prompts, video compliance analysis, and short-form promotional video and audio production.

## Case study
This course uses Cymbal-Mart, a fictional omnichannel retailer with physical stores, warehouses, and e-commerce operations. Learners apply multimodal AI to realistic retail scenarios such as promotions, merchandising quality checks, and social marketing content.
